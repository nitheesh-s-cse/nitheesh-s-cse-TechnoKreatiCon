About the Event

Techno KreatiCon 3.0 is a three-day national-level competition scheduled on October 14, 16, and 17, organised by the Department of Computer Science and Engineering at PPG Institute of Technology.

This flagship event integrates two parallel tracks:

  • Vibe Coding: A test of technical knowledge, creativity, coding skills, and web deployment.
  • Innovation Ideathon: A platform to ideate, innovate, and pitch practical solutions to real-world problems.

Both tracks encourage creativity, design thinking, problem‑solving, and innovation, culminating in a combined grand finale before expert judges.

Complete Structure: From Registration to Final Submissions

Day-wise Schedule

  1. Day 1 • Oct 14 • Online

    Vibe Coding: Submit a pre-developed website for evaluation.
    Ideathon: Submit a PPT of your innovative idea.
    Submissions assessed within hours and results updated live. Ensures a baseline of competence.

  2. Day 2 • Oct 16 • Online

    Vibe Coding: Build to a given theme (e.g., Healthcare, Sustainability, Education) within the time window.
    Ideathon: Receive a real-world problem statement (core engineering). Submit your PPT within the time window.
    Evaluates adaptability, problem‑solving, and creativity under pressure.

  3. Day 3 • Oct 17 • On Campus (PPGIT)

    Vibe Coding: Finalists solve a surprise problem and deploy a functional website in a few hours.
    Ideathon: Final presentations before the jury, followed by a structured Q&A.
    Workshop: Hands‑on vibe coding for all other registered participants.

    Note: Physical presence on Day 3 is mandatory for workshop access, certificates, and rewards. Scorecard is live; jury decisions are final.

General Rules and Instructions

  1. The registration fee is ₹200 per participant, applicable across both tracks.
  2. Team size: 2–5 members.
  3. Register through the official QR code or registration link.
  4. Open to all college students irrespective of discipline or year of study.
  5. Strict adherence to submission deadlines is mandatory.
  6. All submissions must be original. Plagiarism, duplication, or template-only work without innovation leads to disqualification.
  7. Misconduct (offensive/abusive/disruptive behaviour) will not be tolerated.
  8. Respect participants, mentors, judges, and organisers at all times.
  9. Teams violating rules will be disqualified without appeal.

Code of Conduct

Online Etiquette (Day 1 & 2)

  • Be respectful and cooperative in online interactions.
  • Keep video on during evaluation sessions.
  • Avoid offensive or disruptive communication.

On-Campus Conduct (Day 3)

  • Be punctual; report at the specified time.
  • Respect organisers, volunteers, peers, and venue rules.
  • Maintain discipline and healthy competition.

Specific Rules — Vibe Coding Track

  1. Submissions must reflect self-developed concepts; copied templates are prohibited.
  2. Pre-built templates are allowed only with significant custom code. Otherwise, penalties may apply.
  3. Follow professional standards:
    • Clear structure
    • Clean coding practices
    • Proper documentation
    • Responsive UI and good usability
  4. Submit all deliverables (source code, deployment link, docs) within deadlines.

Specific Rules — Innovation Ideathon Track

  1. Use the structured template provided by the organisers.
  2. Your abstract must clearly highlight:
    • Problem Statement
    • Proposed Solution
    • Use Case
    • Potential Impact
  3. Pitch decks must be professional, concise, and within the prescribed format and time.
  4. Final presentations are delivered live in the finale, followed by jury Q&A.
  5. Mockups, prototypes, flow diagrams, or visual aids are strongly encouraged.

Rewards & Recognitions

  • Exclusive Workshop: Access to a hands-on vibe coding workshop on Day 3.
  • Cash Prizes & Trophy: Winners in each track receive exciting cash prizes and a prestigious trophy.
  • Perks for All: Lunch, refreshments, and participant kits on Day 3.
  • Certificates: Issued to all participants physically present on Day 3.

Closing Note

Techno KreatiCon 3.0 is not just a competition—it’s a platform to explore, innovate, and showcase your abilities. By participating, you’ll engage in real-world problem‑solving, expand your technical knowledge, and connect with like‑minded peers.

We look forward to welcoming you to an event where innovation meets execution.
