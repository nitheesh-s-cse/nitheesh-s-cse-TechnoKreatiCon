Why You Absolutely Should Attend Techno KreatiCon 3.0

Techno KreatiCon Phase 1 & 2, held at PPG Institute of Technology, Coimbatore, was a vibrant platform where young innovators pitched groundbreaking ideas addressing real‑world challenges. Projects were judged by industry experts on innovation, feasibility, and market potential, with live updates ensuring transparency. The event inspired students to push technological boundaries.

CyberZen, a student‑driven community at PPGIT, fosters innovation, technical expertise, and cybersecurity awareness. By organizing events like Techno KreatiCon, along with workshops, hackathons, and competitions in coding, AI, and ethical hacking, the club bridges academia and industry, empowering students to thrive in the digital era.

About the Event

Techno Kreaticon 3.0 is a three-day national-level competition organized by the Department of Computer Science and Engineering at PPG Institute of Technology. This flagship event integrates two parallel tracks: Vibe Coding (technical knowledge, creativity, coding, and web deployment) and the Innovation Ideathon (ideate, innovate, and pitch practical solutions to real-world problems). Both tracks foster creativity, design thinking, problem-solving, and innovation, culminating in a grand finale before expert judges.

Rulebook & Guidelines

Step 1: Event Structure

  • Day 1 (Online): Submit website (Vibe Coding) or PPT (Ideathon). Results updated live.
  • Day 2 (Online): Theme/problem announced. Build and submit within time.
  • Day 3 (On Campus): Final challenge, live presentations, hands-on workshop. Physical presence required for certificates and rewards.
  • Judging: Real-time scorecard. Jury decisions are final.

Step 2: General Rules

  • Registration fee: ₹200 per participant.
  • Team size: 2–5 members.
  • Register via QR code or link.
  • Open to all college students.
  • Original work only; plagiarism leads to disqualification.
  • Respect deadlines and event etiquette.
  • Misconduct or rule violations = disqualification.

Step 3: Code of Conduct

  • Online: Be respectful, keep video on, no disruptive communication.
  • On Campus: Be punctual, respect all, maintain discipline and healthy competition.

Vibe Coding Track

  • Self-developed concepts only; copied templates prohibited.
  • Pre-built templates allowed only with heavy custom code.
  • Follow standards: clear structure, clean code, docs, responsive UI.
  • Submit source, deployment link, docs within deadlines.

Innovation Ideathon Track

  • Follow the official presentation template.
  • Abstract must state: Problem, Solution, Use Case, Impact.
  • Professional, concise deck; within allocated time.
  • Final live presentation with jury Q&A.
  • Mockups/prototypes/flows encouraged.
